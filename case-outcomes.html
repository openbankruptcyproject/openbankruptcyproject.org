What happens to cases like mine?

Real outcomes from 37.9 million federal bankruptcy cases (FJC Integrated Database, 2008–2024). Pick your court and chapter.

How we know this. Figures are drawn from the Federal Judicial Center Integrated Database, the official case-level record of U.S. bankruptcy filings, for filing fiscal years 2008–2024, deduplicated to one record per case. "Discharged" and "dismissed" are the court's recorded disposition. A cell with fewer than 30 cases falls back to the national figure and is labeled.

These are historical outcomes, not predictions, and this is not legal advice. Your case depends on facts a chart cannot capture. If you need help, consult a licensed attorney in your state. A project of the Open Bankruptcy Project, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit.
