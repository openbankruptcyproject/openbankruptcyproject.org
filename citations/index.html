About this Hub

The Open Bankruptcy Project maintains a substrate of statute deep-dives and case-pattern datasets. This citations hub consolidates the most important United States Supreme Court precedents that interpret those statutes. Each citation page is built with Schema.org LegalCase markup so search engines and downstream knowledge graphs can index the decision, the court, the date, the citation, and the statute interpreted.

The cases are organized by doctrinal topic. A single decision often touches multiple statutes; the topical placement reflects the decision's primary doctrinal contribution.

Plan Confirmation & Cramdown

  • Bank of America v. 203 N. LaSalle, 526 U.S. 434 (1999)New-value corollary to the absolute-priority rule
  • Till v. SCS Credit, 541 U.S. 465 (2004)Prime-plus formula for Chapter 13 cramdown interest rate
  • RadLAX v. Amalgamated Bank, 566 U.S. 639 (2012)Secured-creditor right to credit bid in sales under a plan
  • Associates Commercial v. Rash, 520 U.S. 953 (1997)Replacement value (not foreclosure value) governs cramdown of retained collateral
  • Hamilton v. Lanning, 560 U.S. 505 (2010)Forward-looking approach to projected disposable income

Discharge & Nondischargeability

  • United Student Aid v. Espinosa, 559 U.S. 260 (2010)Finality of plan-confirmation orders absent timely appeal
  • Taggart v. Lorenzen, 587 U.S. 554 (2019)Objective fair-ground-of-doubt standard for discharge-injunction contempt
  • Husky Int'l v. Ritz, 578 U.S. 355 (2016)Actual fraud under § 523(a)(2)(A) reaches fraudulent transfer schemes
  • Grogan v. Garner, 498 U.S. 279 (1991)Preponderance of the evidence governs § 523 nondischargeability

Jurisdictional & Article III

  • Stern v. Marshall, 564 U.S. 462 (2011)Article III limits on bankruptcy-court final adjudication
  • Wellness Int'l v. Sharif, 575 U.S. 665 (2015)Litigant consent cures Stern problems

Equitable Powers & Procedure

  • Marrama v. Citizens Bank, 549 U.S. 365 (2007)Bad-faith bar to Chapter 13 conversion
  • Law v. Siegel, 571 U.S. 415 (2014)Section 105(a) cannot override express Code provisions
  • Bullard v. Blue Hills Bank, 575 U.S. 496 (2015)Order denying confirmation of a Chapter 13 plan is not a final, appealable order

Property of the Estate & Stay

  • Patterson v. Shumate, 504 U.S. 753 (1992)ERISA pension plans excluded from bankruptcy estate
  • City of Chicago v. Fulton, 592 U.S. 154 (2021)Mere retention of estate property does not violate § 362(a)(3)

Means Test & Professional Fees

  • Ransom v. FIA Card Services, 562 U.S. 61 (2011)Vehicle-ownership deduction available only when debtor has a loan or lease
  • Baker Botts v. ASARCO, 576 U.S. 121 (2015)No fee-defense fees under § 330(a)(1)

Chapter 11 Specialty Issues

  • Florida v. Piccadilly Cafeterias, 554 U.S. 33 (2008)Section 1146(a) stamp-tax exemption available only after plan confirmation
  • Mission Product v. Tempnology, 587 U.S. 370 (2019)Rejection of a trademark license does not rescind the licensee's rights

How to Use This Hub

Each linked page provides the case caption, citation, court, decision date, doctrine, statutes interpreted, and a primary-source link. The body covers the facts, procedural posture, issue, holding, key reasoning, and post-precedent impact, with cross-links into the Open Bankruptcy Project statute substrate where the precedent is cited.

For empirical case-pattern research that draws on these precedents in district-level dockets, see the case-pattern research index. All datasets are licensed CC BY-SA 4.0.
