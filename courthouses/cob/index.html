Section 02About the D. Colo. Bankruptcy Court

The United States Bankruptcy Court for the District of Colorado is one of 94 federal bankruptcy districts in the United States and sits within the Tenth Circuit. It hears Chapter 7, 11 (including Subchapter V), 12, and 13 proceedings, plus adversary proceedings and contested matters under the federal Bankruptcy Code.
