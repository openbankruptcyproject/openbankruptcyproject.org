Privacy Policy

How we handle your data. Short version: we don't sell it.

Data We Collect

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to measure aggregate site traffic. This includes page views, session duration, approximate geographic region, and device type. We do not collect names, email addresses, or personally identifiable information through analytics.

Aggregate event data from Google Analytics is also exported to Google BigQuery for research use. The BigQuery data is used only for internal trajectory analysis and network health reports, is not shared with third parties, and is not used for advertising.

Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, we receive your name, email address, topic selection, and message. This data is processed through Google Apps Script and delivered to our Gmail inbox. We do not share, sell, or distribute contact form submissions to any third party.

Email Subscriptions

Some sites in our network include an optional email subscription form (powered by Formspree). Subscribing requires only an email address. We use this solely to send research updates. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Cookies

We use only first-party cookies set by Google Analytics. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell data. We do not run advertising. We do not collect financial information. We do not use retargeting. We do not share any user data with attorneys, law firms, or legal service providers.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics (GA4) -- traffic measurement
  • Google BigQuery -- aggregate event data warehouse for research use only; not shared with third parties, not used for advertising
  • Google Search Console -- search query measurement (clicks, impressions, rankings on our own pages)
  • Google Apps Script -- contact form processing
  • Formspree -- email subscriptions
  • GitHub Sponsors -- donations (processed entirely on GitHub's platform)
  • GitHub Pages -- hosting

We do NOT use Google Signals, Google Ads integration, User-ID collection, or any cross-device tracking feature. Google Analytics operates in aggregate-traffic-measurement mode only.

Children's Privacy

Our sites are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained per Google's standard retention policies (14 months). Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox indefinitely for correspondence purposes.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data we hold by contacting info@openbankruptcyproject.org. California residents have additional rights under CCPA. EU residents have additional rights under GDPR.
