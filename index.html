What We Do

The bankruptcy system affects millions of Americans every year, but accurate information is hard to find. Most online resources are attorney marketing disguised as education. We are building something different.

🔍

Screening Tools

Free discharge eligibility screeners powered by 4.9 million federal cases from the Federal Judicial Center. No sign-up. No email capture. Just answers.

📊

Empirical Research

Data-driven analysis of Chapter 13 dismissal rates, repeat-filing patterns, and attorney performance across every federal bankruptcy district.

📚

Educational Network

A 30-domain network of plain-language guides to bankruptcy statutes, procedures, and consumer rights. Written for real people, not search engines.

⚖️

Federal Rules Advocacy

We submit data-backed proposals to the Advisory Committee on Bankruptcy Rules to improve transparency and debtor protection in the federal courts.

Federal Rules Engagement

Our empirical research has been accepted by the federal judiciary for formal consideration.

Suggestion 26-BK-3

Accepted March 23, 2026 by the Advisory Committee on Bankruptcy Rules. Proposes mandatory disclosure of attorney-level dismissal and completion rates in consumer bankruptcy cases, supported by analysis of 4.9 million FJC records across all 94 federal districts.

Our Network

Every site in the network is free, ad-free, and open source. Each domain covers a specific section of the Bankruptcy Code or a key consumer protection topic.

1328f.com Discharge eligibility screener -- Section 1328(f). Check whether a prior filing blocks your discharge. 1328f.org Research platform -- attorney analytics, district statistics, and empirical data on Chapter 13 outcomes. automaticstay.org Guide to the automatic stay -- Section 362. What it protects, when it expires, and how to enforce it. 341meeting.org Guide to the 341 meeting of creditors -- Section 341. What to expect and how to prepare. meanstest.org Guide to the means test -- Section 707(b). Understand the income threshold for Chapter 7 eligibility. 523a.org Guide to nondischargeable debts -- Section 523(a). Which debts survive bankruptcy and why. 727a8.com Guide to Chapter 7 discharge bars -- Section 727(a)(8). Time limits between filings. 109g.org Guide to filing eligibility -- Section 109(g). Restrictions after dismissal or voluntary withdrawal. bankruptcymill.org Consumer protection resource on high-volume bankruptcy practices and how to identify them.
