About This Court

The United States Bankruptcy Court for the Western District of Kentucky handles bankruptcy filings for its federal judicial district. Case records are available on PACER for $0.10 per page, or for free on the CourtListener RECAP Archive when contributed by RECAP users or purchased and donated by organizations like the Open Bankruptcy Project.

This index lists 20 cases from the W.D. Ky. that appear on RECAP. Cases are sorted by filing date (most recent first) and paginated at 500 cases per page. Each row links directly to the full docket on CourtListener - no fee, no login, no paywall.
