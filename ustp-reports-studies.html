USTP Reports & Studies: A Source Library

An indexed map of the U.S. Trustee Program’s public reports and studies on the bankruptcy system, with links to the authoritative DOJ sources and to the data pages we built from them. A neutral reference for researchers, journalists, and the public.

Last reviewed 2026-06-08

All documents below are published by the U.S. Department of Justice and are freely available from the USTP’s Reports & Studies page. We link to the primary sources and, where we have compiled the underlying figures into a usable time series, to our own data pages. See our methodology and accuracy policy.
