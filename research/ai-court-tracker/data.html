AI Court Tracker Dataset (CSV)

Comma-separated download of the OBP AI Court Tracker: judicial sanctions, standing orders, and disciplinary referrals in U.S. federal and bankruptcy courts on AI-hallucinated citations. Includes Mata v. Avianca, Park v. Kim, In re Martin, In re Jackson Hospital, and other published orders under Rule 11 and Rule 9011. Companion JSON download at data.json. Free, CC BY 4.0.
