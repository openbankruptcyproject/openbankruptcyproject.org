RECAP: Making PACER Free

Every page we access through PACER becomes permanently free through RECAP. The RECAP archive -- maintained by the Free Law Project -- makes federal court documents freely available to the public. Every docket we pull, every document we download, is automatically contributed back. Our research does not just produce reports -- it expands the public record.
