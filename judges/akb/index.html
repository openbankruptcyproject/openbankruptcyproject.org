Section 02Context

Article I bankruptcy judges are appointed for renewable 14-year terms by the United States Court of Appeals for the circuit in which the district sits (28 U.S.C. § 152), not by the President. This makes bankruptcy-court appointments structurally distinct from Article III district-court and circuit-court appointments. The FJC Biographical Directory is the authoritative national source for appointment dates, prior practice, and education.

OBP’s per-judge pages summarize court-wide context only. The FJC Integrated Database does not publish case totals broken out by judge, so OBP does not assign court-wide totals to individual judges. For court-wide statistics, see the D. Alaska statistics page.
