Frequently Asked Questions

How many domains does the Open Bankruptcy Project operate?

The Bankruptcy Transparency Network operates 75 domains covering bankruptcy statutes, consumer education, pro se resources, credit rebuilding, asset protection, specialty topics, and transparency research. Three sites also offer Spanish-language versions. The network is continuously expanding.

Are these sites free?

Yes. Every domain in the network is completely free to use. There are no advertisements, no attorney referral fees, no intake forms, and no data harvesting. The Open Bankruptcy Project is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit funded entirely by public donations and volunteer labor.
