# Section 1328(f) Findings

391,951 cases with prior filers received discharge with no evidence of 1328(f) verification.
27.4% of all prior filers with known dispositions across 94 federal districts.
Dataset: 4,895,163 FJC IDB cases, FY 2008-2024.

Policy impact: Accepted as 26-BK-3. Cited in Advisory Committee Consumer Subcommittee memo (Tab 4C, April 15, 2026).
