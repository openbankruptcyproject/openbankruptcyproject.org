Why Donate

The Open Bankruptcy Project is a nonprofit building free tools that help people understand the federal bankruptcy system. We run no ads. We accept no attorney referral fees. We collect no user data. Every tool, every dataset, and every page of content is free to everyone -- forever.

Every dollar donated goes directly to keeping this infrastructure running: PACER access fees, domain renewals, and the research databases that power our screening tools. Everything else is volunteer labor.

Where Your Money Goes

We have no employees, no office, and no overhead. Here is exactly what your donation funds:

  • $ PACER access fees -- Federal court records cost $0.10 per page. Screening thousands of docket pages across 94 districts adds up fast.
  • @ Domain registration -- We maintain 75+ educational domains at roughly $10/domain/year, covering every major bankruptcy statute.
  • > Research infrastructure -- Databases, APIs, and data processing tools that power our case screening and analysis.
  • 0 Everything else is volunteer labor -- Code, content, research, design, outreach -- all donated time. Zero dollars go to salaries or overhead.

Other Ways to Help

Share Our Tools
Know someone facing bankruptcy? Share our free screening tools and educational resources. Every share helps someone make a more informed decision.
Use the 1328(f) Screener
Our flagship tool at 1328f.com screens cases for discharge bar violations. More users means more awareness of the problem.
Star Our Repos on GitHub
Visit github.com/ilikemath9999 and star our repositories. It helps others discover the project.
Install RECAP
The RECAP browser extension automatically frees PACER documents you access. It costs nothing and helps open the court system for everyone.
